# BullePin Help

## What is BullePin?

BullePin is a small, private app for groups of friends to plan things together. You create a "Circle" (a private group), invite a few people by email, and post "pins" (events) inside it. Everyone in the Circle can RSVP Yes / No / Maybe.

## Quick start

1. **Sign up** with an email + password (≥ 6 chars) and a display name.
2. From the home screen, tap **Start a Circle**, give it a name and icon.
3. Open your new Circle, tap the gear icon, and use **Invite Friends** to send an email invite to someone who already has a BullePin account.
4. Tap **Pin to Circle** to post an event with a title, when/where, optional notes and a cover photo.
5. Members of the Circle see the pin and tap Yes / No / Maybe.

## Common questions

**My friend didn't get the invite even though I typed their email.**
At the moment, an invite only shows up if your friend already has a BullePin account with that email. Ask them to sign up first, then re-invite. *(We plan to support invitations to email addresses that haven't signed up yet in a future release.)*

**I can't sign in — forgot my password.**
On the Login screen, type your email in the Phone-or-Email field, then tap **Forgot password?**. Firebase emails you a reset link.

**How do I leave a Circle?**
Open the Circle → tap the gear → **Leave Circle**. The Circle owner can't leave their own Circle — they can only delete it.

**How do I delete my account?**
Settings → scroll to the bottom → **Delete account**. You'll be asked to enter your current password. This permanently:
- Deletes every Circle you owned, along with all its pins and RSVPs.
- Removes you from every Circle you joined.
- Erases your profile.
- Deletes your sign-in.

This can't be undone.

**The Apple sign-in / Facebook / Google buttons disappeared.**
We removed third-party sign-in for v1 to ship faster. Email + password is the only sign-in method for now.

**Notifications aren't working.**
Push notifications aren't enabled in this version. You only see new pins and invites when you open the app. Pull-to-refresh on the home screen to load the latest.

## Reporting a problem

Email **support@bullepin.app** with a screenshot if possible. Tell us what you were doing and what happened. We read every message.
