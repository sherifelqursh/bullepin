# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-05-12
**App:** BullePin
**Contact:** support@bullepin.app *(replace with your real email before publishing)*

This Privacy Policy explains what information BullePin ("we," "us," "our") collects and how we use it. By using BullePin you agree to the practices below.

## 1. Information we collect

When you create an account or use BullePin, we collect:

- **Account info.** Your email address, a password (stored hashed by Google Firebase Authentication, never in plaintext), and a display name you choose.
- **Profile info you provide.** Your name, optional phone number you may add in Settings, and any profile photo you upload.
- **Content you create.** Names and descriptions of "Circles" you create or join, pins (events) you post including title/when/where/notes, cover photos you attach, and RSVP responses.
- **Membership data.** Which Circles you belong to, your role (Admin or Member), and invitations you've sent or received.
- **Technical data.** Basic diagnostic information such as device type, app version, and crash logs collected by Apple/Google's standard developer tooling when you install via the App Store or Google Play.

We do **not** collect:

- Real-time location.
- Microphone or camera audio.
- Contacts (we don't read your address book — invites are sent only to email addresses you type in yourself).
- Advertising identifiers.

## 2. How we use information

- To operate the app — sign you in, display your circles and pins, deliver invitations, and process RSVPs.
- To keep the app secure (e.g. preventing account takeovers via Firebase's authentication infrastructure).
- To respond to support requests you send us.

We do **not** use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

## 3. Where data is stored

BullePin runs on **Google Firebase** (Authentication, Firestore database, and related services). Your data is stored on Google Cloud servers, typically in the region you signed up from. Google's own privacy and security practices apply: see <https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy>.

## 4. Sharing with others

Your data is shared **only**:

1. With other members of a Circle you have joined — they can see your display name, profile photo, RSVPs, and pins you posted in that Circle.
2. With Google Firebase as the infrastructure provider.
3. When legally required (subpoena, court order, etc.).

We never share your account credentials.

## 5. Your rights

- **Edit your profile** at any time from the in-app Settings screen.
- **Delete your account** at any time from Settings → Delete account. This permanently removes your account, all Circles you owned (and their pins/RSVPs/members), removes you from Circles you joined, and erases your stored profile. This cannot be undone.
- **Export your data** — email us at the address above and we'll send you a copy of the data tied to your account.
- **Children:** BullePin is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us so we can delete it.

## 6. Security

- Passwords are never stored in plaintext — Firebase Authentication hashes them.
- Connections between the app and our backend use HTTPS / TLS.
- Firestore security rules prevent users from reading or writing data outside their own Circles.

No system is 100% secure; we do our best.

## 7. Changes

If we change this policy materially, we'll update the date at the top and announce the change in the app.
